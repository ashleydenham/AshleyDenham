My Story

Hey — I'm Ashley. I'm based in Adelaide, South Australia, and for the past few years I worked as a registered nurse, mostly in aged care. I loved the people. I learned a lot about patience, leadership, and showing up when things are hard.

But I've taken a break from nursing to focus on AI and tech, and undertake legal training (JD). It's been a big shift — some days exciting, some days humbling. I'm learning to code, tinkering with electronics, studying how large language models work, and slowly building things I find interesting.

I don't have all the answers figured out yet, and that's okay. I'm drawn to the intersection of technology and care — how we can use these tools to genuinely help people, not just move fast. I started Hamily Holdings as a home for the projects and ideas I'm working on.

This little corner of the internet is where I share what I'm up to. Thanks for stopping by.
